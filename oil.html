Valvoline Cummins

PGPL has been mandated as the authorized distributor of Valvoline Cummins India products for generators, transporters, and industrial purposes since 2010.
Operating as 50:50 joint ventures with Cummins India Limited, Valvoline is one of India’s fastest growing lubricant companies that manufacture a wide range of fluids, viz., engine oils, gear oils, radiator coolants, brake fluids, etc. for engines made by various OEMs, including Tata Motors. The company has a strong team of over 400 employees across the country, 50,000 resellers, and 2,000 direct customers.
PGPL provides a complete range of products specializing in automobiles. Along with Valvoline, we are constantly focused to grow the Y-o-Y sales of Oils and Lubricants and providing constant support to our customers.
Valvoline Cummins operates a massive state-of-the-art plant in Ambernath, Mumbai. Set up in 2013, the plant has a production capacity of 120 MML per annum, along with a base oil storage of 6000+ KL, additive storage of 600+ KL and warehouse capacity of 4000 KL. Equipped with advanced manufacturing technology, the plant produces lubricants using select base oils and additives, adhering to Valvoline’s international formulations and standards.
Valvoline marks a nationwide footprint with 24 fully-equipped warehouses across the country. Its warehouse in Manesar, Haryana has an impressive storage area of 50,000 sq. ft, contributing 21% to the total VCPL volume. The facility is capable of catering to a wide market base across Haryana, Delhi, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh.
